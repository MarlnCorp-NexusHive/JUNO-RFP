# Third-party sources

## RFP response section outlines (`rfp-response-templates.json`)

The **Standard / industry** template section titles and guidance text are adapted from the open **RFP Response Template** in:

- [Satyapraveenv/ai-rfp-response-generator](https://github.com/Satyapraveenv/ai-rfp-response-generator) — `templates/rfp-response-template.md`  
- License: [MIT](https://github.com/Satyapraveenv/ai-rfp-response-generator/blob/main/LICENSE) (Copyright (c) 2025 Satya Praveen Vemuri)

We ship a condensed JSON representation and use it only as a **structural outline** for AI-assisted drafting in JUNO; generated Word files are produced by this application.

The **Federal-style** and **Lean** outlines are original shortenings aligned with common US government and commercial proposal practice (public-domain concepts; not copied from a specific vendor document).
